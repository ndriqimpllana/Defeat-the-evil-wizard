Welcome to my text-based RPG game!

---

## ‚ú® Features
- **Playable Character Classes**  
  - **Warrior**: Strong melee attacks and shield defense  
  - **Mage**: Powerful magic spells and barriers  
  - **Archer**: Fast ranged attacks and evasion  
  - **Paladin**: Holy strikes and divine shields  

- **Special Abilities**: Each class has unique moves like Power Strike, Fireball, Quick Shot, Holy Strike, and defensive abilities.  

- **Dynamic Battle System**:  
  - Turn-based combat with detailed stats each turn  
  - Healing, shields, and evasion mechanics  
  - The evil wizard reacts strategically and can regenerate health  

- **Victory & Defeat**:  
  - Custom messages based on remaining health and battle performance  
  - Multiple outcomes to make each playthrough exciting
